About Me

From a young age, I've always been fascinated by the intricate dance of code and technology. This curiosity led me to pursue a Master of Science in Computer Science at Texas State University, where I graduated in December 2020 with a concentration in Full-stack Software Development. My journey in the tech world has been driven by a passion for solving real-world problems and designing automated software solutions that make a difference. Over the past 4+ years, I've honed my skills as a software engineer, diving deep into the realms of FinTech, EdTech, Railway, Critical Infrastructure, and Defence industries. Each project I've undertaken has been a stepping stone, enhancing my ability to develop and customize robust web applications. My roles have not only sharpened my programming and problem-solving skills but also reinforced the importance of continuous learning and adaptation in this ever-evolving field. Collaboration is at the heart of my approach. I thrive in team environments where ideas flow freely, and innovative solutions are born. My experience with cutting-edge programming tools and emerging technologies has empowered me to drive projects forward, ensuring that they meet and exceed business needs. As I continue to navigate this dynamic landscape, I remain open to new opportunities and challenges. My story is one of relentless curiosity, technical expertise, and a commitment to creating scalable, impactful solutions. After all, "learning is a continuous process," and I'm excited to see where this journey takes me next.
